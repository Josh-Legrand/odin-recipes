Tacos

Desciption

A taco is a traditional Mexican food consisting of a small hand-sized corn- or wheat-based tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Chilli pepper
  • Tomato
  • Ground beef
  • Shell
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Select shell of choice(soft or hard)
  2. Cook and season your ground beef
  3. Season the meat
  4. Chop some onions up
  5. Add lettuce(chopped or whole)
  6. Combine all inside shell
  7. Enjoy!
